Most of that time was spent on the island of Maui, but I worked 2 summers out of Seward, Alaska as well.
Recently I made to move to upstate New York due to family concerns. As there is no boating in the area, I was forced to make a career change, and as there are no interesting jobs in the area, I decided to pick up a skill I could use from anywhere. After some debating, coding seemed like the solution, and after some searching, Coding Temple seemed like the way to go.
